# Behavioral: Conflict Resolution (STAR Method)
Category: Behavioral/Conflict Resolution

## Question: Describe a time you had a conflict with a colleague. How did you handle it?

### Situation
Provide context. e.g., "Working on the I3 transition, a teammate and I disagreed on the database schema choice for the new audit module."

### Task
Define the challenge. e.g., "The goal was to reach a consensus that balanced performance with scalability without delaying the sprint."

### Action (The "Meat")
What DID you do?
1. Stayed objective (data over feelings).
2. Set up a dedicated 15-min sync to walk through both designs.
3. Identified common ground (security requirements).
4. Suggested a POC (Proof of Concept) for the disputed section.

### Result
Metric or outcome. e.g., "The POC proved the teammate's approach was faster for reads. We adopted it, the sprint delivered on time, and our working relationship improved due to the transparent process."
